Discover Bohol – Website Plan

Site Name

Discover Bohol

The name was selected because it clearly communicates the purpose of the website: to help visitors explore and learn about Bohol’s attractions, culture, and travel opportunities. It is simple, memorable, and tourism-focused.

Site Purpose

The website serves as an informational travel guide to Bohol, Philippines. It provides visitors with key destination highlights, cultural insights, food recommendations, and practical travel information such as transportation, weather, and best times to visit.

Scenarios

  • What are the must-visit attractions in Bohol for first-time travelers?
  • When is the best time of year to visit Bohol and what is the weather like?
  • How do I get to Panglao Island and what activities can I do there?

Color Scheme

#2A6F97
#F4D35E
#2E8B57

#2A6F97 (Ocean Blue) – Used for header, navigation, and accents to represent the sea.

#F4D35E (Sand Gold) – Used for highlights and background sections to reflect beaches and the Chocolate Hills.

#2E8B57 (Palm Green) – Used for buttons and secondary accents to represent nature and eco-tourism.

Typography

Playfair Display – Used for headings to create an elegant travel-magazine feel.

Open Sans – Used for body text for readability and accessibility.
